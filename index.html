Lobster Bisque Recipe

A rich, creamy, and luxurious seafood soup

Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cook Time: 45 minutes
Serves: 4-6
Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 2 lobster tails (about 1 lb total)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup cognac or brandy
  • 4 cups seafood stock
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the lobster

    Remove lobster meat from shells and cut into chunks. Reserve shells for stock.

  2. Make the base

    Heat olive oil in a large pot. Saut√© onion, carrots, and celery until softened, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add aromatics

    Add garlic and tomato paste, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.

  4. Deglaze

    Add cognac and cook until alcohol evaporates, about 2 minutes.

  5. Add stock and simmer

    Add lobster shells, stock, bay leaf, and thyme. Simmer for 20 minutes.

  6. Strain and finish

    Strain the soup, return to pot. Add cream and lobster meat. Simmer for 5 minutes.

  7. Final touches

    Whisk in butter, season with salt and pepper. Garnish with chives and serve hot.

Chef's Tips

  • Use fresh lobster shells for the best flavor
  • Don't boil the cream - it may curdle
  • Strain the soup well for a smooth texture
  • Serve immediately for best taste
